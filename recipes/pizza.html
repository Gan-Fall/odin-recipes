Pizza

Description

A classic delish. Especially recommend a hawaiian style.

Ingredients

  • Dough (Assuming you already know how to make it)
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, or marbled cheese
  • Pineapple, fresh or cooked (optional)
  • Cooked Bacon (optional)
  • Sweet thai honey garlic sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Drop some tomato sauce on top of your dough and spread it evenly.
  2. Shred or sprinkle your cheese in a generous amount on top of the sauce. Add your toppings.
  3. Assuming you just want a cheese pizza pop it in the oven or bbq on top of a griddle at 475 degrees fahrenheit for 10-15 minutes. I recommend bbq if you want the crust to be tougher and less chewy.
  4. If you added the bacon and pineapple for a hawaiian style, add your sweet thai honey garlic sauce now to truly ascend.
