Spaghetti

Description

A classic italian recipe. Quick, simple, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Sugar (Optional)
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Main course of your choice
  • Your favorite blend of italian spices, especially basil

Steps

  1. Prepare the main course and optionally the sauce with the spices if you'd like.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil and pour a bit of salt in it.
  3. Cook the pasta according to the package directions.
  4. Serve the pasta and pour the sauce over it, adding a healthy amount of parmesan on top.
